1. Responsive Webpage

Responsive web design is an approach to web development that aims to create websites that provide an optimal viewing experience across a wide range of devices, from desktop computers to mobile phones. The goal of responsive web design is to ensure that a website's layout and content adapt seamlessly to different screen sizes and orientations, providing users with a consistent and user-friendly experience regardless of the device they are using.
